Projects Department

Finding real world solutions

Over here at the Project Department of SEDS Antariksh, we try to instil a sense of interest and excitement among students about space and astronomy. Through our various technical projects and events, we try to build a community of students who can cater to the futuristic needs of the space and aerospace industry.

We follow the cycle of learning, teaching, experimenting and building cool stuff to develop a community with strong technical expertise and interest in the field of aerospace. Your love for innovation, creation and space is all that it takes to become a member of our community.
